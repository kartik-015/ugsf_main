# TrackMate Pro - Website Overview

**TrackMate Pro** is a comprehensive student management portal specifically designed for Charusat University's engineering departments (CSE, CE, IT). The website serves as a centralized digital platform that streamlines academic operations by connecting students, faculty, administrators, counselors, and HODs through role-based dashboards and real-time communication features. 

Built with Next.js 14 and MongoDB, the platform enables students to submit assignments and track grades, allows faculty to manage courses and evaluate submissions, empowers administrators to oversee registrations and system operations, and provides HODs with project approval and guide assignment capabilities. 

The website transforms traditional paper-based academic processes into an efficient digital ecosystem, featuring automated email validation, real-time notifications, comprehensive progress tracking, and secure file management to enhance educational outcomes across all engineering disciplines at the university.
